Cheers!

Manning’s is the friendly neighborhood bar right down the street. We take pride in our great tasting beer – we clean the lines every night! Signature cocktails include everything from the standard Bloody Mary to a crisp Martini. Enjoy a glass of wine on the patio or stop in for drink specials during happy hour. Since 1932 Manning’s has been the place on Como for good times.
